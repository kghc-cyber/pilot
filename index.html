How to Wear Your Bra Liner

1

Position Hold the liner with the center curved peak pointing upward.

2

Prepare Put your bra on, but leave the band slightly loose.

3

Insert Tuck the liner directly under the bottom edge (band or underwire) of your bra.

4

Center Ensure the liner sits flat against your skin, with the center peak resting between your breasts.

5

Secure Fasten and adjust your bra as usual to hold the liner in place.
